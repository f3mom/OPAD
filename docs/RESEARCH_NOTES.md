# Research notes used for OPAD Mega

OPAD Mega folds together ideas from public A/D CTF tooling:

- Traffic analysis: Packmate, Tulip, Pkappa2, Shovel/Suricata, Caronte.
- Capture fanout: pcap-broker and PCAP-over-IP.
- Exploit farms/distribution: DestructiveFarm/S4DFarm, ExploitFarm, Ataka, Neo, CookieFarm, CTFPWNng.
- Defense filters: ctf_proxy and YAMPA.
- Game/checker models: FAUST CTF Gameserver, EnoEngine, ForcAD.
- Submitter concepts: flagWarehouse and farm queues.

These tools differ by deployment, APIs and CTF rules. OPAD therefore renders safe plans and adapters first, then leaves live apply to explicit team-controlled configuration.
